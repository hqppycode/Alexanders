Hero Text

Every bride wants their dream wedding. Unfortunately, most of the time money stands in the way for making that dream a reality. At least, that's the way it was for Mike and Tina Alexander when they were planning their wedding in 1990. They did not have the money to pay someone for an elaborate wedding, so instead they made their dream wedding happen themselves. They planned everything from the food to the decor. It turned out beautifully and the guests loved it! On May 5, 1990, the day of their wedding, Mike decided he wanted to help other brides make their dream wedding a reality, thus, Alexander's the Great Events was created. And for 25 years the goal has not wavered: to make every event a great event!
